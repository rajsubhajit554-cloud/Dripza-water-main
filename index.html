FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order starts from 50 bottles. We offer great wholesale pricing for bulk orders of 250+ bottles.

How much time does delivery take?

Typically 5–7 working days after design approval and advance payment. Urgent orders may be arranged on request.

Is the label waterproof?

Yes! All our labels are printed on premium waterproof material so they stay intact even in wet conditions.

Is the design service free?

Yes, free design is included with all our packages. Just share your logo or ideas and our team will create it.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

We require an advance payment before production starts. The remaining balance is paid upon delivery of the product.

Can I see a sample before ordering?

We send you a digital design proof for approval before printing. Physical samples may be available for large orders.
